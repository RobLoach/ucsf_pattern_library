About

Leading the Way

The leading university exclusively focused on health, UC San Francisco is driven by the idea that when the best research, the best education and the best patient care converge, great breakthroughs are achieved.

News & Media

UCSF News Office

The News Office oversees media relations for all University programs and patient services. Each member of our team of public information professionals specializes in a wide array of beats in patient care, research discoveries and education -- and connects reporters with top experts. Explore the resources below and sign up to receive our news first via RSS.
